Logan Square Cooperative

(Gallery View to open on click)

Living type: units with private kitchen and bathrooms

Size: 8 units

Neighborhood: Logan Square

Main intersection: Diversey and Pulaski (at 3934 West George Street, Chicago, Illinois, 60618)

Building type: multifamily 5-10

Website: https://www.facebook.com/logansquarecoop

Shared common spaces: Lush garden and basement storage. New back porches. Close to public transport, park and free swimming pool just a block away; garage space is rotated for $50 a month.

Shared utilities: Solar system for hot water installed, laundry facilities.

Our Motto: Given the present economic crisis, this is your way to save money, live in better conditions, take a stand for affordable housing, and build community.

Governance: Our criteria for membership include: Agreement with the principles of unity; commitment to affordable housing and living; willingness to make a commitment to living cooperatively by dedicating time and skills to cooperative work and activities; effectively communicating with members; commitment to stay long term in LSC; has skills that would add to LSC existing skills, abilities and interests; agreement on process of consensus decision making and willingness to learn and practice this process.

Equity Structure: Our Coop is a limited equity coop because we are committed to long-term affordable housing. We have saved a large building from for-profit developers, and we are encouraging others to try the same. If a member moves out, (s)he only would take the amount put toward principal on the mortgage, downpayment, and major improvements (s)he put in that have been approved by the coop- that defines limited equity.

Established: 2001
