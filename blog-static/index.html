Why should you care?

Social impact is an emerging field that continues to evolve. Everyone has their own idea of what it is or how it should be done. RESUS believes we shouldn't try to box it in ... instead we should keep it open to new frameworks. Maybe it shoudn't even be a field at all and simply a quality inherent in existing fields. We share our thoughts occaisionnally about this topic here.
