How can we help you?

Want to learn if cooperatives are a good fit for you?

  • Use a tool to make it easy to consider co-ops with friends - Fireplace.
  • See examples of architecturally-designed yet affordable co-op units.
  • Find out how to visualize your ideal housing situation with graphics.

Are you currently living in a cooperative?

  • List your available co-op unit.

  • Get financial and legal resources.
  • Become a network member to add your co-op profile.
