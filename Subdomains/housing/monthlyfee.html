What do I pay every month?

It's a combo.

Every month, you will likely pay a combination of group costs and independent costs.

Group costs, or your "monthly fee," include your contribution towards group financing (Initial Costs) and group expenses for the building (Ongoing Costs).

On top of these group costs, you might need to account for independent costs. These include services like electricity, internet, or extra amenities you use that other members don't use. Some groups choose to include these services in their monthly fees.

Why are we doing this?

We are not pleased with the two dominant options for housing in America: renting and owning ... and we think a lot of people would agree. We believe there is room for a third option.

We've been working on housing initiatives for a while, and we genuinely think Shared Equity / Property Ownership has potential to provide decent, affordable housing for all.
